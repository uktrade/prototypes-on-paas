Trade Remedies Authority

Trade Remedies Service

The UK Trade Remedies Authority (TRA) investigates if trade remedies are needed to prevent injury to UK industries caused by unfair trading practices or unforeseen import surges.


Active cases

We publish active cases so everyone can keep track of ongoing cases.

View active cases on the public file

Using the Trade Remedies Service

You can use the Trade Remedies Service to:

  • request a new trade remedies investigation or review
  • participate in an active trade remedies case
  • invite an organisation or individual to represent you in an active case
  • add a colleague to your account or an active case

You will need to create an account to use the service. Please have any registration details for your organisation ready. It takes about 10 minutes.
